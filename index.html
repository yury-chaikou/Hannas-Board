Defying conventions
in everyday

Coatue launched in 1999 with $50 million in assets under management and a clear goal: to partner with visionary entrepreneurs from around the globe and invest in the next generation of category-defining, transformative technologies that will drive efficiency and create entirely new industries.

In the 20 years since, we have spotted incredible opportunities amid architecture shifts in technology, alongside the transformative power of data. Staying ahead of theses shifts, balanced by smart risk assessment, has allowed Coatue to weather two financial crises and grow approximately $18 billion in total assets under management.
